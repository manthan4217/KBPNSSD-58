Green Army Initiative

“Plant Today, Protect Tomorrow” 🌱

About Green Army

The Green Army Initiative is one of the most impactful and long-standing environmental programs of our NSS Unit. Launched in 2018, this initiative aims to develop environmental awareness, responsibility, and sustainable habits among student volunteers.

A unique feature of this initiative is that every student enrolling in NSS is required to plant at least one tree at the beginning of their journey. This practice not only promotes afforestation but also builds a personal connection between the volunteer and the environment.

With approximately 300 students enrolling each year, the initiative has grown consistently, contributing significantly to increasing green cover both within and beyond the campus. Volunteers are also encouraged to take care of the planted trees, ensuring their growth and survival.

In addition to individual plantation, large-scale tree plantation drives are regularly organized in collaboration with Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC), where volunteers actively participate in creating greener urban spaces and spreading awareness about environmental conservation.

The Green Army Initiative is not just about planting trees—it is about nurturing responsibility, promoting sustainable living, and inspiring students to become environmentally conscious citizens.

🌿 Since 2018, more than 2700 trees have been planted by NSS volunteers.

🌍 Cleaner Environment

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, helping create a cleaner and healthier atmosphere.

💧 Water Conservation

Plantation helps maintain groundwater levels and prevents soil erosion during heavy rains.

🌱 Sustainable Future

Every tree planted today contributes towards a greener and safer tomorrow for future generations.

Green Army Activities

🌱 Mandatory Tree Plantation

Every NSS volunteer plants a tree at the time of enrollment, making environmental responsibility a core value from the very beginning of their NSS journey.

  • 🌿 One student = One tree initiative
  • 🌍 Builds personal responsibility towards nature
  • 🌱 Encourages long-term care and growth of trees

This practice ensures continuous contribution to environmental sustainability every academic year.

🌳 Plantation Drives with NMMC

Tree plantation drives are regularly conducted in collaboration with Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC), focusing on increasing urban greenery and spreading environmental awareness among the community.

  • 🏙️ Urban plantation initiatives
  • 🤝 Government collaboration (NMMC)
