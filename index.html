About NSS Unit D-58

The NSS Unit D-58 of Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil College, Vashi has been actively serving the community since 2010. The unit aims to develop students' personality through community service while promoting leadership, discipline, and social responsibility.

The unit actively works in areas such as environmental conservation, education support, health awareness, and social welfare. Volunteers regularly participate in initiatives like Mangroves Cleanup Drive, Green Army, One Step for Education, and Mayechi Ubb.

Students from various streams actively engage in NSS activities, gaining practical experience, teamwork skills, and a deeper understanding of societal needs.

Through consistent efforts, the unit has made a positive impact on local communities by spreading awareness and encouraging responsible citizenship.

The unit is also widely recognized for organizing one of the largest Shiv Jayanti celebrations among colleges across Navi Mumbai, promoting unity, culture, and leadership.
