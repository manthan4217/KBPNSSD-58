Mangrove Cleanup Drive

“Protecting Nature, Preserving Future” 🌊🌿

About Mangrove Cleanup Drive

The Mangrove Cleanup Drive is a flagship environmental mission of NSS Unit D-58 dedicated to restoring and protecting the fragile coastal ecosystems of Navi Mumbai.

🌊 Every Sunday, volunteers step into mangrove zones to remove plastic waste, bottles, debris, and harmful pollutants that threaten marine biodiversity.

🌿 Mangroves are not just trees — they are natural shields that protect our coastline from floods, storms, and soil erosion while supporting thousands of marine species.

🤝 This mission is conducted in collaboration with Environment Life Foundation and supported by Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC), ensuring real environmental impact on the ground.

👨‍🏫 Under the visionary guidance of Founder Dharmesh Barai Sir, students learn discipline, responsibility, and the importance of environmental leadership.

💚 What makes this initiative unique is its consistency — it is not a one-time activity but a continuous environmental movement driven by student passion.

🌱 “We don’t just clean nature — we heal it, protect it, and stand for it.”

Our Field Experience 🌱

Every Sunday morning begins with boots, gloves, and determination. Volunteers travel to mangrove zones where they face muddy terrain, plastic waste layers, and strong coastal conditions — yet continue with full dedication.

What starts as a cleanup activity turns into a life-changing experience — students understand the real condition of our environment and their responsibility toward it.

Many volunteers say this is the most impactful NSS activity because it connects them directly with nature.

Key Activities

🌊 Weekly Cleanup Drives

Every Sunday, NSS volunteers actively participate in mangrove cleanup drives to remove harmful waste and protect coastal ecosystems.

  • ♻️ Plastic, glass & debris collection
  • 🌿 Protection of marine ecosystem
  • 🤝 Team-based field work

🧹 Waste Segregation & Disposal

Collected waste is carefully segregated into recyclable and non-recyclable categories to ensure proper disposal and minimal environmental impact.

  • 🔍 Sorting waste on-site
  • ♻️ Recycling support
  • 🚛 Safe disposal practices

📢 Awareness & Education

Volunteers educate the public about the importance of mangroves and reducing plastic usage.

  • 🚫 Reduce plastic usage
  • 🌍 Environmental awareness
  • 🗣️ Community interaction

🤝 Collaboration & Guidance

Drives are conducted with Environment Life Foundation and NMMC, with guidance from Founder Dharmesh Barai Sir.

  • 🏛️ NMMC collaboration
  • 🌿 NGO partnership
  • 👨‍🏫 Expert motivation

🌱 Ecosystem Protection

The initiative helps restore mangrove habitats and protect biodiversity from pollution and human impact.

  • 🐟 Protect marine life
  • 🌊 Prevent coastal damage
  • 🌿 Preserve biodiversity

💪 Volunteer Engagement

The drives build teamwork, discipline, and social responsibility among NSS volunteers.

  • 👥 Team coordination
  • ⏰ Regular participation
  • 🌟 Leadership development
