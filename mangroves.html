Mangrove Cleanup Drive

“Protecting Nature, Preserving Future” 🌊🌿

About Mangrove Cleanup Drive

The Mangrove Cleanup Drive is one of the most impactful environmental initiatives carried out by our NSS Unit. This activity is conducted every Sunday with dedication and enthusiasm by our volunteers.

The drive is regularly organized in collaboration with Environment Life Foundation and also supported by Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC).

A special source of motivation for our volunteers is Founder Dharmesh Barai Sir, who continuously guides us about environmental values, reducing plastic usage, and the importance of protecting mangroves.

Mangroves play a crucial role in protecting coastal ecosystems. They prevent erosion, support biodiversity, and act as natural shields against storms and floods.

During the cleanup drives, volunteers collect plastic waste, glass, and other harmful materials from mangrove areas. The collected waste is then properly segregated and sent for recycling or safe disposal.

Along with cleaning, awareness is spread among the community about environmental protection and sustainable living practices.

🌱 Together, we are protecting nature and building a cleaner future.

Key Activities

🌊 Weekly Cleanup Drives

Every Sunday, NSS volunteers actively participate in mangrove cleanup drives to remove harmful waste and protect coastal ecosystems.

  • ♻️ Plastic, glass & debris collection
  • 🌿 Protection of marine ecosystem
  • 🤝 Team-based field work

🧹 Waste Segregation & Disposal

Collected waste is carefully segregated into recyclable and non-recyclable categories to ensure proper disposal and minimal environmental impact.

  • 🔍 Sorting waste on-site
  • ♻️ Recycling support
  • 🚛 Safe disposal practices

📢 Awareness & Education

Volunteers educate the public about the importance of mangroves and reducing plastic usage.

  • 🚫 Reduce plastic usage
  • 🌍 Environmental awareness
  • 🗣️ Community interaction

🤝 Collaboration & Guidance

Drives are conducted with Environment Life Foundation and NMMC, with guidance from Founder Dharmesh Barai Sir.

  • 🏛️ NMMC collaboration
  • 🌿 NGO partnership
  • 👨‍🏫 Expert motivation

🌱 Ecosystem Protection

The initiative helps restore mangrove habitats and protect biodiversity from pollution and human impact.

  • 🐟 Protect marine life
  • 🌊 Prevent coastal damage
  • 🌿 Preserve biodiversity

💪 Volunteer Engagement

The drives build teamwork, discipline, and social responsibility among NSS volunteers.

  • 👥 Team coordination
  • ⏰ Regular participation
  • 🌟 Leadership development
