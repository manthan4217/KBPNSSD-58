NSS Special Residential Camp 2026

7 Days of Service, Leadership & Growth

Village Development Camp

Empowering Rural Communities through Youth Action

📅 Date: 25 Dec – 31 Dec 2025 📍 Location: Shree Chatrapati Shivaji Vidyalaya, Wavanje, Tal. Panvel, Dist. Raigad 📅 Duration: 7 Days 👥 Volunteers: 150+

📅 Daily Activities

Day 1 – Inauguration & Camp Setup

Date: 25 December 2025

  • All volunteers assembled at the college at 8:00 AM and prepared for departure.
  • A traditional pooja ceremony was conducted in presence of Dr. Shubhada Nayak Madam, Dr. Pranita Bhale Madam, Mr. Amit Surve Sir, and Mr. Nitin Gamare Sir.
  • The camp was formally initiated with coconut breaking for शुभारंभ.
  • Volunteers reached Wavanje and began camp setup activities.
  • Cleaning of kitchen, dormitories, washrooms, and arrangement of water facilities was completed.
  • Official Inauguration Ceremony held at 4:00 PM with dignitaries: Shri G. R. Patil, Dr. Shubhada Nayak, Shri M. R. Patil.
  • Volunteers divided into groups: Red, Green, Yellow, Blue, White.
  • Responsibilities assigned: Admin, Food, Cleanliness, Cultural, Shramdaan.
  • White Group: Dinner preparation.
  • Red Group: Cultural program on “Not Me But You”.
  • Day ended with documentation and reflection writing.

Day 2 – Awareness & Discipline

Date: 26 December 2025

  • Morning started with prayer, exercise, and Zumba session.
  • Blue Group: Admin duties (stage, rangoli, sound setup).
  • Red Group: Breakfast preparation.
  • White Group: Shramdaan (school & ground cleaning).
  • Yellow Group: Cleanliness (rooms, utensils, washrooms).
  • Lecture on “Green Entrepreneurship” by Shri Devvrut Jadhav Sir.
  • Session on “Vyasanmukti” by Shri Uday Tilave Sir.
  • Debate on “Economy and Reservation”.
  • Green Group: Cultural program on “Unity in Diversity”.
  • Daily documentation and reflection completed.

Day 3 – Social Service & Awareness

Date: 27 December 2025

  • Morning exercise and Zumba conducted.
  • White Group: Admin duties.
  • Green Group: Breakfast preparation.
  • Blue Group: Cleanliness maintenance.
  • Red Group: Shramdaan – garden development and plantation.
  • Cleaning of water filtration system for safe drinking water.
  • Organized Eye Check-up Camp for school students.
  • Lecture by Ethical Hacker Mr. Vishnu Nemane on Cybersecurity.
  • Debate on “Government vs Public Responsibility”.
  • Yellow Group: Cultural program on “Addiction”.

Day 4 – Rights & Social Awareness

Date: 28 December 2025

  • Morning session conducted with exercise and Zumba.
  • Red Group: Admin responsibilities.
  • Yellow Group: Food preparation.
  • White Group: Cleanliness duties.
  • Green Group: Shramdaan for campus development.
  • Lecture on “RTI and Democratic Rights” by Adv. Prof. Rajendra Madhavi Sir.
  • Interaction with former NSS volunteers.
  • Debate on “Social Media”.
  • Blue Group: Cultural program on “Sustainable Development”.

Day 5 – Environment & Career Awareness

Date: 29 December 2025

  • Morning exercise session conducted.
  • Green Group: Admin duties.
  • Blue Group: Breakfast preparation.
  • Red Group: Cleanliness duties.
  • Yellow Group: Shramdaan – village lake cleaning.
  • Lecture on “Career Guidance” by Mr. Satish Pawar Sir.
  • Debate on “Government Corruption”.
  • White Group: Cultural program on “Health & Fitness”.

Day 6 – Field Experience & Reflection

Date: 30 December 2025

  • Morning exercise and Zumba session.
  • Yellow Group: Admin duties.
  • White Group: Breakfast preparation.
  • Green Group: Cleanliness duties.
  • Blue Group: Shramdaan – visit to brick-making unit.
  • Awareness program at Adivasi Pada on Drug De-addiction.
  • Activities mainly handled by FY volunteers.
  • Lecture by Dr. Rajshri Ghorpade and Shri Sakharam Pote Sir.
  • Final cultural night by SY volunteers and campfire.

Day 7 – Valedictory & Conclusion

Date: 31 December 2025

  • Final morning session with exercise and Zumba.
  • Blue Group: Admin duties.
  • Red Group: Breakfast preparation.
  • Yellow Group: Cleanliness duties.
  • White Group: Shramdaan – BP Check-up Camp with Wavanje Hospital.
  • Valedictory Ceremony conducted.
  • Awards Distributed:
    • 🏆 Blue – Best Group
    • 🏆 Red – Best Cleaning
    • 🏆 Green – Best Discipline
    • 🏆 Yellow – Best Food
    • 🏆 White – Best Cultural
  • Experience sharing by volunteers.
  • Camp concluded with group photos and return journey.
