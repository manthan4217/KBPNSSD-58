Camp Schedule Overview

Day 1 – Orientation & Inauguration

Inauguration ceremony, NSS briefing, group formation, campus introduction and cleanliness drive.

Day 2 – Awareness Activities

Yoga session, cleanliness drive, cyber security lecture, and cultural program activities.

Day 3 – Community Service

Area cleaning, door-to-door awareness, health survey, and guest lectures.

Day 4 – Health & Environment

Eye checkup camp, soil testing awareness, debates and cleaning activities.

Day 5 – Social Development Work

Water conservation work, plantation drive, and awareness lectures.

Day 6 – Cultural & Leadership

Cultural events, leadership training, group activities and reflection sessions.

Day 7 – Closing Ceremony

Final cleaning drive, certificate distribution, feedback and closing ceremony.
