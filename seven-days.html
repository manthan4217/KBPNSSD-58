NSS Special Residential Camp 2026

7 Days of Service, Leadership & Growth

Village Development Camp

Empowering Rural Communities through Youth Action

📅 Date: 25 Dec – 31 Dec 2026 📍 Location: Shree Chatrapati Shivaji Vidyalaya, Wavanje, Tal. Panvel, Dist. Raigad 📅 Duration: 7 Days 👥 Volunteers: 150+

📅 Daily Activities

Day 1 – Arrival, Inauguration & Orientation

The NSS Special Residential Camp began with the arrival of volunteers in the village. The camp was formally inaugurated in the presence of respected dignitaries.

  • Inauguration ceremony with chief guests
  • Introduction of NSS objectives and camp goals
  • Group formation and duty allocation
  • Explanation of daily schedule and discipline rules

Day 2 – Routine Activities & Cleanliness Drive

The day started with morning assembly followed by physical exercise. Volunteers were assigned duties based on their groups.

  • Morning prayer, NSS song and exercise (Yoga – Yellow Group)
  • Cleaning drive in village (Red Group)
  • Shramdan for school surroundings (White Group)
  • Lecture on "Cyber Security" by Mr. Vishnu Nemane
  • Debate on "Ladki Bahin Yojana"
  • Cultural program on "Nasha Mukti" (Blue Group)

Day 3 – Awareness & Youth Development

Volunteers actively participated in social awareness and administrative duties.

  • Shramdan (Green Group): Village cleaning & eye checkup awareness
  • Administrative duties (White Group)
  • Cleaning (Yellow Group) and Food distribution (Blue Group)
  • Lecture on "Youth Participation in Building Viksit Bharat" by Dr. Kalpesh Sawant
  • Presence of Dr. V.B. Pujari & Dr. Gajanan Dhandekar
  • Debate on "Electric Vehicles vs Fossil Fuel Vehicles"

Day 4 – Health Awareness & Social Discussion

The day focused on health awareness and continued village development activities.

  • Shramdan (Blue Group): Eye checkup campaign, cleaning & soil testing
  • Cleaning (White Group), Food (Red Group), Admin (Green Group)
  • Lecture on "Eye Checkup Awareness" by Dr. Prashant Thorat
  • Guests: Mr. K.N. Ghadge & Mr. Umesh Bagal
  • Debate on "Is it necessary for youth to go abroad?"
  • Cultural program on "Family is True Strength" (Yellow Group)

Day 5 – Shramdan & Social Awareness

This day was dedicated to physical work and social awareness activities.

  • Shramdan (Red Group): Construction of Bandhara (small dam)
  • Cleaning (Green Group), Admin (Blue Group), Food (Yellow Group)
  • Lecture on "Stories in Social Work" by Ms. Vaishali Magdum
  • Guests: Dr. Harsha Goyal, Dr. Manisha Abhyankar, Mr. Pankaj Dandage
  • Debate on "Shraddha vs Andhashraddha"
  • Cultural program on "Disadvantages of Alimony" (White Group)

Day 6 – Cultural Activities & Awareness

Volunteers showcased their talents through cultural performances and awareness programs.

  • Street plays (Nukkad Natak) on social issues
  • Dance and drama performances
  • Public awareness activities
  • Preparation for closing ceremony

Day 7 – Valedictory Function & Closing

The final day marked the conclusion of the camp with formal closing activities.

  • Morning prayer, exercise and final assembly
  • Stage setup and camp arrangements (Blue Group)
  • Valedictory function with guest speeches
  • Certificate distribution to volunteers
  • Sharing of camp experiences
  • Vote of thanks and emotional farewell
