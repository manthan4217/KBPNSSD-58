Shiv Jayanti Celebration Week

NSS Unit Activity Report

📌 Overview

The NSS Unit celebrated the week of Shiv Jayanti with great enthusiasm and unity. Various social, cultural, and community-based activities were organized throughout the week to spread the values and inspiration of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj among students and society.

📅 Week Activities

During Shiv Jayanti week, Shiv Paryan was conducted continuously for seven days with active participation from volunteers and students. A Blood Donation Camp was also organized in the college to encourage social responsibility and help needy patients. Along with this, several meaningful activities such as an Old Age Home Visit, awareness programs, and social service activities were conducted.

🎉 Main Day Celebration

On the main day of Shiv Jayanti, all NSS volunteers gathered together and started the celebration with an energetic Lezim performance, creating a traditional and enthusiastic atmosphere in the college campus.

After the opening procession, a grand cultural program was organized in the auditorium. Every year, a special theme is decided for the celebration, and students perform various acts, dances, and cultural presentations based on that theme.

🏹 Message

The program highlighted the bravery, leadership, and ideals of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and inspired students with the message of unity, courage, and social service.

🤝 Conclusion

The entire celebration was successfully carried out with teamwork, discipline, and active participation from volunteers, teachers, and students, making the Shiv Jayanti week memorable and meaningful for everyone.
