A Story of Warmth

Maayechi Ubb is not just a project — it is an emotion of care, where old sarees transform into blankets of hope for those in need.

About the Project

The Maayechi Ubb project is a heartfelt initiative carried out during the Shiv Jayanti week by NSS Unit D-58.

The main aim of this project is to help people living on the streets who struggle during cold weather conditions without proper shelter or warmth.

As part of this initiative, each volunteer contributes two used sarees from their home. These sarees are then stitched together by volunteers to create warm and usable blankets.

After preparing the blankets, volunteers visit nearby areas and distribute them to homeless and needy people living on roadsides and open spaces.

This simple effort not only provides warmth but also spreads kindness and care towards people who need it the most.

❤️ A small act of kindness can bring warmth to many lives.

Key Activities

🧵 Collection of Sarees

Volunteers collect used sarees from their homes to contribute towards making blankets.

🪡 Stitching Blankets

Sarees are stitched together by volunteers to create warm, reusable blankets for distribution.

🚶 Field Visits

Volunteers visit nearby areas to identify homeless individuals living on streets and in open spaces.

🤝 Blanket Distribution

Prepared blankets are distributed directly to needy people, providing them warmth and comfort.

Locations Covered

Through this initiative, we were able to provide warmth to several people who live without proper shelter during cold nights.

The project also helped volunteers understand the real-life struggles of people on the streets and encouraged a sense of compassion and responsibility towards society.

Though the effort is small, it brings comfort and hope to those in need.

More Information

🎯 Objective

To provide warmth and dignity to homeless individuals during harsh weather through recycled saree blankets made by volunteers.

🤝 Community Participation

The project encourages active participation from students, building empathy, teamwork, and social responsibility.

♻️ Sustainability

By reusing old sarees, the initiative promotes eco-friendly practices and reduces textile waste.

🌍 Social Impact

The initiative not only provides physical warmth but also emotional support to underprivileged people.

Our Vision

We aim to continue this initiative every year during Shiv Jayanti week and involve more volunteers in the process.

By collecting more materials and reaching more areas, we hope to extend this effort to help a larger number of people in need.

❤️ Warmth shared is humanity served.
