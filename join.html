Instructions Before Enrollment

  1. All volunteers must complete 120 hours of NSS work in the first year.
  2. In the second year, NSS work continues under Field Project.
  3. NSS certificate is given after 2 years + 1 Special Camp.
  4. Participation in one Special Camp is compulsory.
  5. Marks are given based on performance and participation.
  6. Regular attendance is mandatory.
  7. Discipline and teamwork must be maintained.
  8. Participation in social awareness and community service is required.
  9. Balance academics with NSS activities.
